    MuseumLog is an iPhone app for keeping a beautifully illustrated log of every museum and gallery you visit — with photos, ratings, exhibitions, companions, and the favourite piece you saw. Think of it as Letterboxd, but for art.

    Open the app on the way home, tap Add Visit, pick the museum (we have a built-in atlas of the big ones), drop your photos in, and rate it. The whole entry takes about thirty seconds.
